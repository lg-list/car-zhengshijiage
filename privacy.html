Privacy Rules

Purchase documents must be masked before display. Contributors should hide names, phone numbers, addresses, full VIN, loan account numbers and payment details. AutoLedger keeps only fields that help shoppers understand market pricing.

Allowed Fields

Model year, brand, model, trim, state, transaction month, MSRP, discount, taxes, fees, add-ons and final out-the-door price.

Prohibited Fields

Any personally identifying or account information, including full VIN, driver license, address, phone, email, bank card, loan account number and signature.
