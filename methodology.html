Data Methodology

AutoLedger labels price evidence in three levels: masked receipt, public price signal and pending owner submission. Receipt images are only shown after personal details are hidden and key transaction fields remain readable.

Fields We Use

Model, trim, model year, city, state, MSRP, dealer quote, discount, taxes, DMV fees, dealer add-ons, source level and final out-the-door price.

Fields We Do Not Show

Names, phone numbers, addresses, full VIN, financing account details, dealer contact names, payment account numbers and any direct personal identifiers.

Public Sources

Public forums, automotive research sites and OEM configuration pages are used as leads. Before launch-quality display, records should be reviewed and labeled by source confidence.
